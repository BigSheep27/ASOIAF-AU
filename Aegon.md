Aegon “Egg” Targaryen VI

Parents: Rhaegar Targaryen & Elia Martell

Age: 18 (299 AC)

Height: 6’2

Physique: lean-muscle and chiseled physique. Broad shoulders.

Face: much like his father, Aegon is inhumanly beautiful. He has that melancholic beauty his father had (even if it is completely opposite of Aegon’s personality) that make women weep. He has been described as “ethereally, hauntingly beautiful”.

Hair color: silver

Hairstyle: shoulder length hair in a half up fishtail braid, with two thin braids with gold caps falling over each side of his face framing it.

Eyes: haunting, beautiful, deep indigo irises. His eyes were naturally heavy-lidded which people interpreted either as “bedroom eyes” or “sad eyes” even if it was neither. He just looked Like That.

Normal everyday clothing:

- Black and red sherwani
- churidars
- boots
- a golden circlet where the ends meet at the forehead and curve upwards. It has a red ruby in the middle. Originally belonged to Princess Elia Martell. Saved by Ashara Dayne and given to Aegon.

In battle:

- Red surcoat with a black dragon on its lefts chest
- black pauldrons
- black vambraces
- black, clawed gauntlets
- Black greaves

Weapon:

Blackfyre - The Sword of Kings. Previously wielded by Aegon The Conqueror and most notably, Daemon I Blackfyre. A bastard sword. It has a dark grey blade with rippled patterns. The blade is wider than a typical longsword or bastard sword. Its crossguard are two golden dragons snarling with a bronze dragon wing as its rainguard. Its grip is wrapped in black leather with a gold ring around it in the middle. It has a bronze diamond-shaped pommel with a red ruby in the middle.

Personality:

He is charismatic and playful. Aegon is a fun-loving, witty young man who loves banter. However, Aegon has some hedonistic tendencies. He frequents pleasure houses and sleeps with random beautiful women who are swayed by his beauty, Ashara (the one he views as his current mother) says it is a miracle he hasn't fathered dozens of bastards yet. Aegon also loves to drink and fight. He considers his unrivaled prodigious talent in the sword as a core piece of who he is. Aegon is highly social and loves engaging with people no matter their standing in life. Has that cocky charm but does his best to be honorable, even if Ashara has to give him a clout in the ear sometimes. Despite wanting to be honorable, Aegon has no problem with resorting to ruthless means, as in his own words “I break things but I plan to leave it better than I found it”. With Ashara being in his corner, Aegon is a hard man to steer. Aegon values freedom above all and has been known to help the smallfolk, peasants, and slaves of Essos. Aegon is incredibly content with his life. He has his brothers-in-arms, his mother Ashara, Rhaenys and her fat cat, Ser Balerion. Even if he knows he has a claim to the Iron Throne through his father Rhaegar, he does not have immediate goals of conquest, he only dreams of bringing his family home. Never having any real desire towards the Iron Throne. Unlike most others who romanticize his father, Aegon deeply resents his father and his memory, viewing him as a deeply terrible man who left his mother Elia alone, and them (himself and Rhaenys), to kidnap a Stark girl who was barely a woman grown. Though he resents his father, the resemblance is undeniable. Aegon is even talented with a harp, which he loves to play for his mother Ashara and his sister Rhaenys. Harbors a deep, simmering rage for the Lannisters who has his mother raped and murdered, as well as plotted to kill them. Unlike Rhaenys who is much more open and curious about their mother Elia despite her fragmented memories, Aegon never talks or asks about Elia.

Background:

While Ashara saved Rhaenys’s innocence and managed to keep Rhaenys the sweet, witty young girl she was who loved to sing and dance, Aegon grew up obsessed with swords from Ashara’s stories of her brother, Arthur Dayne. For Ashara who grew up around Arthur, it was extremely easy to see that Aegon was a prodigy with a sword. With all the wealth she gained from selling Dayne heirlooms and jewelry, she had Aegon taught by knights and even waterdancers just to broaden his horizon. Unknown to Ashara, at age 14, Aegon entered a tourney where sellswords fought in training squares and mercenary compounds for money. Aegon won decisively and won a large amount of money from the people betting against him. Ashara was terrified but because it brought in money, and it wasn't a duel to their deaths, Ashara let him continue. A year later, Lysono Uncovers the exiled prince. A mysterious, stunning Westerosi woman (Ashara) living in hiding with a silver-haired, purple-eyed boy and a beautiful young girl who looks Dornish through and through, instantly caught his attention. Lysono investigates them and uncovers the truth of the baby swap and realizes this boy is Aegon VI, the undeniably legitimate heir to the Iron Throne, and the maiden is Rhaenys, Aegon’s older sister. Lysono realizes that the Golden Company’s current leadership is stagnant, waiting for a “perfect moment” or a Blackfyre plan that may never come. In Aegon, Lysono sees a legitimate Targaryen prince who can inspire loyalty within the Golden Company and bring them home. Another one of Lysono’s concerns; By 298 AC, the Golden Company had a massive internal crisis: the original exiles were dead. The older generation fought for a home they remembered. The younger generation (like young recruits or Essosi-born fighters) are just mercenaries with no emotional tie to Westeros. Lysono Maar recognizes that if the Company doesn’t find a plan to sail to Westeros soon, they will just become another nameless Essosi sellsword band. They need a cause now to stay relevant. Aegon is that cause. However, Lysono doesn’t just hand Aegon a crown; he tests him. Lysono secretly arranges for Aegon to enlist in the Golden Company under a false name (Egg). For three years, Aegon bleeds in the mud of the Disputed Lands. He earns the respect of the brutal Westerosi exiles, not because of his royal blood, but because he is a lethal fighter and a natural leader. When Lysono and Ashara finally reveal his true identity to the high command, it isn’t an abstract claim. The officers look at this 18-year-old warrior who has saved their lives in battle and realize: This isn’t a fragile prince we have to protect. “This is our Captain-General  who can lead us across the sea.” By 18, Egg was named Captain-General and replaced Harry Strickland. During the ceremony, Harry presented the Valyrian Steel Sword Blackfyre to Aegon. Aegon has been wielding it since.

WHY follow a Targaryen?

- Aegon as Bittersteel’s Ideal

Bittersteel founded the Golden Company because he hated the weak, bookish, sorcerous Targaryens (like King Aerys I and Bloodraven). He wanted a king who was a warrior, a leader of men, and a true soldier.

- Because Egg has spent three years fighting in the ranks, he embodies everything Bittersteel actually respected. When Lysono reveals Egg’s identity to the veteran captains, he doesn’t show them a pampered prince in silk. He shows them an 18-year-old commander covered in sweat and dynamic battlefield scars, who drinks their sour wine and knows how to lead a cavalry charge. The Blackfyre traditionalists look at him and realize: “He may have the Targaryen name, but he has a Blackfyre’s warrior soul.”

Relationship with Jon Connington:

Because of his relationship with Rhaegar, Jon expected he would be beside his dear prince’s side, however, it did not go well for Jon. Aegon RESENTED Jon because he sees Jon as everything wrong with Westeros. Why his father and men similar to his father end up going the paths they go because men like Jon enable them. When Jon tried to get close to Aegon, Aegon simply let him know that his mother Ashara had let him know how Jon treated his biological mother, Elia. Seeing her as weak, unworthy of Rhaegar, and was snobbish, Aegon simply told Jon "You see my father when you look at me. But I am Elia Martell’s son. If you cannot respect her blood, you have no place at my table." Aegon has kept Jon at a distance since.
