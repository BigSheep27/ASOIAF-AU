The Golden Company consists of 10,000 knights, Westerosi exiles, Summer Islanders, and free city natives. The 10,000 are split into:

- 1,000 archers with Black Balaq as their commander. Broken into 333 Crossbowmen, 333 Eastern Bowmen (using recurve bows), 234 Longbowmen (uses massive, heavy yew longbows), and 100 Summer Islanders all wielding Goldenheart Bows (the elite vanguard. Considered the best archers in the known world. Carries cutlasses as their second weapon)
- The Cavalry of 500 knights and 500 squires all with their own horses and have Ser Jon Connington as their commander.
- The Infantry (8,000 men). Unlike standard Westerosi foot soldiers who are mostly peasant levies, the Golden Company's infantrymen are highly disciplined professional mercenaries. They are primarily spearmen and pikemen who fight in highly synchronized, unbreakable phalanx formations. They carry swords and axes, but those are secondary weapons for close-quarters chaos after the lines clash. Ser Tristan Rivers is their commander.

24 War Elephants (not counted as part of the 10,000 men) - often given names based on puns and understatement. Aegon’s main elephant is Ser Stomps-a-Lot. Rhaenys’ favorite one is Lady Peanut.

Top Ranking officers

Aegon Targaryen - The Captain-General

Ser Harry Strickland - The Paymaster. Often called Homeless Harry. A balding man with pale skin and has a big gut (often called a Walrus by Aegon). Notoriously unglamorous for an ex captain-general of a legendary Sellsword Company. He is cautious and risk-averse, has more of a financial mindset, pragmatic and unenthusiastic for battle, and surprisingly quirky. He has a deep, genuine fondness for elephants. Always complains about his bunions. Middle aged man in his late 30s or early 40s.

Lysono Maar - the spymaster. Straight, long golden-blonde hair with violet eyes and a feminine appearance. Flamboyantly dressed and elegant. In conversation, he is noted to be glib, smooth-talking, yet tightly guarded, often speaking at length without actually revealing valuable information to outsiders. With The Golden Company, he is much more open and theatrical. Comparing himself to a “beautiful” court jester. Appears to be mid 20s.

Black Balaq - Commander of the archers. Dark skin. Thick, short, curly white hair. Proud, pragmatic, and disciplined. The one who always keeps a leveled head. In his late 60s. Grooming Maelo Xo to take over as the next commander of the archers.

Ser Tristan Rivers - Commander of the Infantry. Westerosi exile. Long hair often in a pony tail, lean and wiry build. He is observant, genial, and deeply dutiful. In his early 30s.

Ser Jon Connington - Commander of the Cavalry. Unlike the rest of the high ranking officers, he’s the one who Aegon speaks to the least. 40 years old.

Ser Denys Strong - secret descendant of the ancient, extinct, noble house, House Strong. intimidator and interrogator. As tall as Aegon 6’2 but has a wider build. Bald head. Boisterous like his younger brother.

Aegon’s closest friends:

Aegon has a great relationship with most of the high ranking officers but his closest friends are the ones closest to him in age and they all rose up in ranks around the same time (Aegon’s hard work and drive motivated the younger squires and eventually got knighted).

Ser Rolly “Duck” Duckfield - standing at 6 feet, and has a lean-muscular athletic frame, short & curly amber hair. Knighted by Ser Jon Connington. Honorable, playful, approachable and protective. Loves to jape with Aegon. 20 years old.

Ser Franklyn Flowers - the bastard of Cider Hall. Franklyn has aristocratic features and is surprisingly handsome. Straight, long dark hair, bright brown eyes. Franklyn is a neat freak and is obsessed with being a gentleman and a proper knight. He’s the one who tries to keep everyone courtly, only to get laughed at because they are sellwords. Tries to keep Aegon from calling everyone “cunts”. 21

Ser Duncan “Dunk” Strong - a secret descendant of House Strong. 6’8. Brolic. Short hair styled in a caesar cut. Loud and boisterous. Wields a 6 foot, flanged mace. Loves to get drunk with Aegon. Thinks that Aegon’s nickname (Egg) and his (Dunk) are incredibly ironic and has since then called himself Lord Commander of Egg’s Kingsguard. 21. Younger brother of Ser Denys.

Maelo Xo - dark skin. Neck length, white hair in locs styled in a manbun with the sides shaved. Bright brown eyes. Handsome and charming. About the same height as Aegon but with a less muscular bjt lean build. Playful, mischievous, loves to drink and visit pleasure houses, highly social. The Golden Company jokes that Aegon and Maelo themselves have kept the pleasure houses of Tyrosh in business. Maelo is only second to Black Balaq in archery, and wields double cutlasses. 18

Haldon the Halfmaester - short and neat light brown hair. Athletic build despite being a maester. Clad in a maester’s robe. Haldon earned this nickname because he studied at the Citadel of Oldtown but never forged enough links to earn a full maester's chain. Despite leaving before completion, he possesses advanced knowledge in history, languages, geometry, and medicine. Deeply gentle, charming, and optimistic. Despite being a halfmaester, Haldon also trained under a waterdancer and is an extremely good fighter who hides curved daggers under his robes. Despite his talent for fighting, Haldon is deeply devoted and spiritual. His faith provides him with a strong moral compass, and makes him highly empathetic.
