Rhaenys “Rhae” Targaryen

Parents: Rhaegar Targaryen & Elia Martell

Age: 19 (299 AC)

Height: 5’6

Physique: possesses a tall, leggy, and high-waisted frame that gives her a commanding presence. She features an hourglass silhouette with thick thighs.

Hair color: dark brown

Hairstyle: long, thick, voluminous hair falling in ringlets

- silk scarf draped across her body

Personality:

Rhaenys is a sweet, gentle, playfully mischievous, young lady with a sweet wit who loves to sing and dance. She is a fiercely devoted sister to Aegon and daughter to Ashara. Rhaenya knows that even though she is older by a year and a half, Aegon is more of the older sibling, but it doesn’t stop her from supporting Aegon in his endeavors. Rhaenys carries a quiet guilt about Ashara keeping her sheltered early on in her life to keep her innocence while Aegon was in the disputed lands fighting and bleeding. Rhaenys takes initiative by helping her mother in her mercantile business and takes over for Aegon when he is away. Rhaenys is intelligent and takes after Ashara’s grace. Unlike Aegon who never talks about their biological mother Elia, Rhaenys is much more open and asks Ashara questions about Elia. On peace times (when the Golden Company don’t have any contracts), Rhaenys’ favorite hobby is singing and dancing while her brother plays the harp during campfires. Another one of her hobbies is giggling and rolling her eyes with Ashara at women who weep over Aegon’s beauty. Considering that while Aegon knows he’s an attractive man, he does not know HOW attractive he is exactly. Aegon could be at a tavern calling Harry Strickland an “absolute walrus”, women would still weep. It is one of Rhaenys’ favorite forms of entertainment.

Combat knowledge - Rhaenya hates anything to do with blades. According to her, she’s too pretty to fight. However, Rhaenys is a very skilled archer. Aegon gave her a Goldenheart bow and a rondel dagger to keep herself safe just in case.

Ser Balerion the fat, lazy cat - Rhaenys’ pet and a gift from Aegon. Ashara had told Aegon that when they were still in Westeros, Rhaenys had a black cat as a pet she named Balerion, named after the most fearsome dragon in the history of Westeros, claiming it would one day grow into a very large and terrifying dragon. Rhaenys was so heartbroken as a child when Varys couldn't save Balerion and send it along with her to Dorne that Rhaenys grew sick and frail as a child. On Rhaenys’ 18th name day, Aegon came home bruised and bloody holding a black kitten. Aegon saved it from a group of drunkards abusing it and took it home to Rhaenys. It was love at first sight. Rhaenys hugged Aegon and cradled the kitten, immediately naming it Ser Balerion. She talks to it like it can understand her and she can understand it.

Relationship with Jon Connington - unlike her brother Aegon who is dismissive and cold towards the exiled knight, Rhaenys remains kind and sweet to Jon, serving as a bridge. This forces Jon to face his prejudice against the late princess Elia because the son of his beloved prince hates him, but the daughter of Elia, who is the spitting image of Elia but healthier and more radiant, is kind to him.
